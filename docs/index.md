## What This Project Ships

Desktop Pet Mitarashi is a lightweight Electron app that keeps a cat mascot visible without taking over your workspace. The app is designed to stay out of the way: it lives in the tray, can start hidden after login, and lets you bring the mascot back instantly when you want it.
